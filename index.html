Learning Versatile Humanoid Manipulation with Touch Dreaming
Yaru Niu, Zhenlong Fang, Binghong Chen, Shuai Zhou, Revanth Senthilkumaran, Hao Zhang, Bingqing Chen, Chen Qiu, H. Eric Tseng, Jonathan Francis, Ding Zhao
ArXiv Preprint 2026, Under Review by International Conference on Intelligent Robots and System (IROS)
abstract | webpage | arXiv
We present Touch Dreaming, a framework for learning versatile humanoid manipulation by incorporating tactile sensing into a world-model-based reinforcement learning pipeline. Our approach enables humanoid robots to perform dexterous tasks requiring precise contact sensing, achieving robust generalization across diverse manipulation scenarios.

Towards Autonomous Robot-Assisted Minimally Invasive Surgery: a Dexterous Wristed Robotic Kit (DWRK) for Surgical Tasks
Qiming Wu, Xinbo Chen, Binghong Chen, Fanghao Wang, Sibo Hao, Mingchuan Zhou, Limin Zeng
Under Review by International Conference on Intelligent Robots and System (IROS) abstract
We introduce DWRK, a dexterous wristed robotic kit designed for autonomous robot-assisted minimally invasive surgery. DWRK provides a modular, low-cost platform with high dexterity for performing surgical manipulation tasks, enabling data-driven learning approaches toward full surgical autonomy.

STTRL-DVO: Transformer-based Reinforcement Learning for Robust Dynamic Target Tracking in Cluttered Environment
Fanghao Wang*, Binghong Chen*, Youchao Zhang, Xiangyu Guo, Yining Lyu, Chuanjie Liu, Alois Knoll, Di Cui, Huanyu Jiang, Yibin Ying, Mingchuan Zhou
IEEE Transactions on Robotics (TRO), 2026
abstract | code
We propose STTRL-DVO, a transformer-based reinforcement learning method for robust dynamic target tracking in cluttered environments using direct visual odometry. Our spatiotemporal transformer captures long-range dependencies across frames, enabling reliable tracking of fast-moving targets under occlusion and visual clutter.
